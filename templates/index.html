Recent controversy surrounding Riot Games.

Several current and former employees have recently been interviewed by the popular online news source, Kotaku, about alleged gender discrimination at Riot Games headquarters. Five current and former employees have even filed a lawsuit against them for this very matter. However, Riot has filed motions to force two of them into private arbitration, making it much harder for the employees to seek justice. Due to this, 150 employees staged a large scale walkout.
