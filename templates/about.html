What is this website?

What is the purpose of the website? Why did I make it?

This site hopes only to reiterate the facts surrounding the alleged gender discrimination at Riot Games. It is made as part of a project for my GSTR 410 class.
Using this site, I explore the controversy itself, as well as several factors that I may believe may influence or are influenced by it. These include the professional E-sports side of the game, the streaming community that surrounds it, and the actual game itself, as well as all the champions and cosmetics availabe therein.
To reiterate, this site is not intended to villainize Riot Games, but instead to enlighten those that do not know much about the issue.
